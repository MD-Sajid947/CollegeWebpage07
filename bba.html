Degree Awarded

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

About the Program

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at XYZ College is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business principles and practices. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for the dynamic business world.

The program focuses on developing essential skills in management, leadership, communication, and critical thinking. Students gain exposure to various business functions including marketing, finance, human resources, and operations.

With a blend of classroom learning, case studies, industry projects, and internships, our BBA program ensures that graduates are well-equipped to enter the corporate world or pursue higher studies.

Admission Process

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • 10+2 mark sheet and passing certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Transfer certificate from previous institution
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Character certificate from previous institution
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhar card, passport, etc.)

Selection Process

Admission to the BBA program is based on:

  1. Academic performance in 10+2 (minimum 50% aggregate)
  2. Performance in personal interview
  3. Written aptitude test (if applicable)
