Our Story

Welcome to GEORGE COLLEGE OF MANAGEMENT & SCIENCE

The George Telegraph Training Institute was established in 1920 to arrest the rising tide of unemployment by providing job oriented training, tailored to the requirements of industry.

In 2003, George College expanded its educational domain by establishing The George College of Management & Science with a campus near Taratala, Maheshtala, and Budge Budge Trunk Road in South West Kolkata, also affiliated to WBUT (currently known as MAKAUT) for conducting full-time Bachelors Degree Courses in various professional fields.

At GEORGE COLLEGE OF MANAGEMENT & SCIENCE, we believe in educating the whole person. Our holistic approach to education emphasizes not just academic achievement, but also character development, leadership skills, and social responsibility.

Our Core

Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that prepares students to be ethical leaders, critical thinkers, and engaged citizens in a global society through rigorous academics, innovative research, and meaningful service.

Vision

To be recognized as a premier institution of higher learning that empowers students to achieve their full potential and make significant contributions to their professions and communities.

Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Diversity, Community, and Lifelong Learning. These core values guide our decisions, shape our culture, and define our institutional character.
