Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM)

A 4-year professional degree program designed to develop future leaders in the global hospitality industry through comprehensive training in hotel operations, food production, service management, and business administration.

Apply Now

Program Overview

About the Program

The Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM) is a comprehensive undergraduate program that prepares students for managerial positions in the hospitality industry. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical training in all core areas of hotel operations including front office, housekeeping, food production, food & beverage service, and hospitality marketing.

Program Highlights

  • 4-year degree program with industry internship
  • State-of-the-art training kitchens and restaurants
  • Industry-experienced faculty
  • International exposure opportunities
  • 100% placement assistance
  • Specialized certifications included

Learning Outcomes

Graduates will develop competencies in hotel operations, customer service, event management, food safety, hospitality accounting, and human resource management. The program emphasizes leadership development, problem-solving skills, and cultural sensitivity to prepare students for global hospitality careers.

International Opportunities

Our BHM program offers international exchange programs with partner institutions in Europe, Asia, and North America. Students can participate in semester abroad programs, international internships, and global certification courses to enhance their cross-cultural competencies and global employability.

Specialization Options

Food Production

Master culinary arts, kitchen management, and international cuisines with hands-on training in our professional kitchens.

Food & Beverage Service

Learn restaurant operations, bar management, and fine dining service techniques in our training restaurant.

Accommodation Operations

Specialize in front office management, housekeeping operations, and property management systems.

Event Management

Develop expertise in planning and executing conferences, weddings, and corporate events.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent examination from any recognized board
  • Minimum aggregate marks of 50% (45% for reserved categories)
  • Candidates must have completed 17 years of age at the time of admission
  • No prior hospitality education required
  • Good communication skills in English preferred
  • Personal interview may be conducted for final selection
