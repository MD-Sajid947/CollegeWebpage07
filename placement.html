Our Placement Process

1

Registration & Profile Creation

Students register with the placement cell and create their professional profiles highlighting skills, projects, and achievements.

2

Training & Preparation

Comprehensive training including technical skills, aptitude tests, group discussions, and mock interviews.

3

Company Presentations

Recruiters visit campus to present their company profiles, job roles, and expectations to students.

4

Application & Shortlisting

Students apply to companies of their choice and companies shortlist candidates based on profiles.

5

Selection Process

Companies conduct tests, interviews, and other evaluation methods to select candidates.

6

Offer & Onboarding

Selected students receive offers and are guided through the onboarding process by the placement cell.
