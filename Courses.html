Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Undergraduate Degree
3 Years (6 Semesters)
School of Business
₹75,000 per year

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at University of Technology is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business principles and practices. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications to prepare students for leadership roles in various business sectors.

Program Highlights

  • Industry-aligned curriculum with case study approach
  • Specializations available in Marketing, Finance, and HR
  • Summer internships with leading corporations
  • Guest lectures from industry experts
  • Entrepreneurship development programs

Curriculum Structure

  • Principles of Management 4 Credits
  • Business Economics 4 Credits
  • Financial Accounting 4 Credits
  • Business Mathematics 3 Credits
  • Marketing Management 4 Credits
  • Organizational Behavior 3 Credits
  • Business Communication 3 Credits
  • Human Resource Management 4 Credits

Undergraduate Degree
3 Years (6 Semesters)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program provides students with a strong foundation in computer science and information technology. The curriculum covers programming languages, database management, web development, and software engineering, preparing students for careers in the IT industry.

Program Highlights

  • Hands-on training in latest technologies
  • Industry projects and internships
  • Specializations in AI, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing
  • State-of-the-art computer labs
  • Hackathons and coding competitions

Curriculum Structure

  • Programming in C 4 Credits
  • Data Structures 4 Credits
  • Database Management 4 Credits
  • Operating Systems 3 Credits
  • Web Technologies 4 Credits
  • Object-Oriented Programming 4 Credits
  • Software Engineering 3 Credits
  • Computer Networks 4 Credits
